One Month Away!
We've had a busy and exciting month following the announcement of our partnership with the University of Texas, and we want to continue that enthusiasm and momentum into the 2014 Austin Challenge! The Challenge is just over a month away, and we already have over 1,300 participants that have decided to fight with us, but we think we can do better! My challenge to you is this: recruit your friends, family, neighbors and coworkers to participate in the Challenge!
With just a month to go, there is no better time than now to set your fundraising goals and start achieving them! Remember, every donation counts and helps us offer free programs and services to those affected by cancer. If you don’t know where to start, check out our Fundraising HQ for tips and suggestions.
As always, feel free to contact us if you have any questions. I hope you are looking forward to an exciting and memorable day at the Challenge!
Callie Atkins
